My name is Nick Hess. I am a New York City based software engineer and technology consultant.

Born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA, I graduated from Duquesne University with a degree in finance. Though I enjoyed and found success within the finance program, my passion has always been in the constantly evolving tech world. Through a combination of both formal and non-traditional schooling, I decided to focus my career on web development, supplementing my existing knowledge of computer science fundamentals with the languages, frameworks, and design patterns used in modern websites and applications.

I am currently employed full-time as a software developer with the Mental Health Association of New York City, primarily working with Python and .NET stacks built on an AWS-based cloud architecture. I have previously worked as a Creative Development Engineer(front-end) and Product Development Specialist(full-stack) at Spotible Inc. in New York, and as a Web Application Developer Intern at Urban Innovation 21 in Pittsburgh. Though I am not currently looking for a new full-time opportunity, I am available for freelance roles.
